XYZ Normal Mapping

In the Phong Shader, we implemented XYZ Normal Mapping so long as the material had an xyzNormal component and the uv coordinates of map were provided. We took the RGB value of the normal map and converted this into calculation of the normal at that given point. We followed the guidelines on the specs in order to implement XYZ Normal Mapping.
